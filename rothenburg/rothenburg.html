Rothenburg ob der Tauber - A Christmas Dream

A visit in December

This little town in Bavaria, not far from the bigger and more famous Nurenberg is a real dream for lovers of medieval history and during Christmas time it turns into paradise for kids -- and christmas lovers of all ages.

Although a visit is worth all year long just to admire the beauty of this small and well preserved city, we came here in december specially for the Christmas Market, so I will describe our two-day trip and how the city looks like at this time of the year
